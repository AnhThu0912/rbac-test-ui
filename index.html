WHO ARE WE?

RMIT Business Analytics Champion (RBAC) is a distinguished national student competition that was first established in 2020. This competition provides a dynamic and enriching platform for passionate undergraduate students to deepen their knowledge and gain practical experience in the field of Business Analytics. Additionally, RBAC facilitates valuable connections between prestigious sponsors and their prospective customers, interns, and various other stakeholders.

rules and
regulation
Eligibility
  • All nationalities are accepted
  • Participants must be undergraduate students and currently studying in universities in Vietnam (Students enrolling in universities outside Vietnam are not eligible)
  • The former Top 5 finalists of RBAC previous seasons, Ambassadors, and Organizers are not allowed to participate in the competition
Registration
  • A team must consist of 4 members (each contestant can only join one team, and students from different universities could form a team).
  • Each contestant can join only one team.
  • If any team wants to change its member(s), the team leader must inform the organizers before the registration form closes. If the request is sent after the form deadline, that team must continue with at least 3 members.
  • Participants can use National ID/Passport, Student Card or Statement of Enrolment for identification.
Notes
  • Teams must strictly comply with the regulations and deadlines set by the Organizers for submissions of each round.
  • All sessions (including rounds, workshops, and pitching round) are held in English. All team submissions must be in English
  • RBAC organizers reserve the right to disqualify any team if that team shows signs of violation or does not comply with the above rules (Organizers will contact the team at least 12 hours before making the final decision on a disqualification).
